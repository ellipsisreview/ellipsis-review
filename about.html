About us

"Ellipsis: marks or a mark (such as … ) indicating an omission (as of words)”

  Ellipsis is a literary publication that aims to find and develop the omissions made by the ellipses of modern culture. This publication hopes to make these ellipses develop and mature into complete ideas that the reader will carry forever. This is done by delving into the best literature of the past, and considering insights from writers of the present. In sharing the fruits of these endeavors, there is a hope that the reader may develop the ellipses in their own lives, thereby living by the Socratic maxim "The unexamined life is not worth living".

Sincerely,
Desmond Jones
Founder and editor of Ellipsis
